ChainWhales is a 100% Hand drawn collection on the Ethereum blockchain. We are building a community of gamers that will be able to play against other community members in order to win ETH rewards during “The Whale Games”. Only ChainWhale holders and holders of our future 3D collection drop will be able to participate in The Whale Games. Check out or Whale Plan!

3D NFTs are on the way! Tune into our twitch to watch the artist Whale Willy creating these.


Whale Plan - The Whale Games

50% of the Whale Sales will be put into The Pod Treasury and given back to Whale holders as ETH rewards in exclusive game nights that will be planned by the Whales. These game nights for ETH rewards will only be accessible as a Whale holder (2D Collection Holder or 3D Collection Holder). These games may include individual games or team-based games. The winner of these games will be rewarded with the predetermined ETH reward that will be stated when the game night is announced. Games may include Call of Duty Warzone, Valorant, Fall Guys, GangBeasts, Marbles on Stream, League of Legends, Rainbow Six Siege. Whale Holders may vote on the game during the planning stages of these nights. Game nights are also open to suggestions of more games that the community can play for ETH rewards. There will also be games hosted for non-Whale holders of the community to play in as well. These games will not be for ETH rewards, but they may be playing for WL of our 3D collection.

Whale Plan - 3D Collection

Our Current 2D hand drawn collection holders will be voting on the amount of Genesis Whales that will be raffled out for purchase. These 2D Whale holders will be given whitelist for our 3D collection drop to be named by the 2D Whale Holders. Our Lead Artist Willy will be streaming the creation of this 3D collection on his twitch channel frequently and will announce when he going to stream in our discord channel if you are interested in watching the creation of 3D NFTs. Release date will be released in our discord and updated here in the coming weeks.

More on the Whale Plan to be released in our discord!
